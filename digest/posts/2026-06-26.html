OpenAI has decided to delay the release of its latest model, GPT-5.6, following a request from the Trump administration over security concerns. This decision highlights the growing tension between AI innovation and regulatory oversight, as governments become increasingly wary of the potential implications of advanced AI models. The delay will see GPT-5.6 initially shared with a select group of partners rather than a public release.
