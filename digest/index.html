Pentagon Labels Anthropic a Supply-Chain Risk

The Department of Defense has officially labeled Anthropic as a supply-chain risk, marking the first time an American AI company has received such a designation. This move could have significant implications for Anthropic's business operations and its relationship with government contracts, especially as CEO Dario Amodei attempts to negotiate terms to salvage a $200 million deal. The situation highlights the growing tension between AI companies and government oversight, potentially setting a precedent for future interactions.
