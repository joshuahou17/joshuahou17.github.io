Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Split

How accurate is the receipt scanning?
Our AI is trained on millions of receipts and achieves 95%+ accuracy for most restaurant receipts. It works best with clear, well-lit photos and standard receipt formats. For any items it misses or misreads, you can easily tap to edit or add them manually. The more you use Split, the better it gets at recognizing your favorite restaurants.
What payment methods are supported?
Split works with all major payment platforms: Venmo, Apple Pay, Cash App, PayPal, Zelle, and Google Pay. When you're ready to collect payments, Split automatically generates the right payment link for each person based on their preferred method. No need to ask "What's your Venmo?" - we handle it all.
Is my data private?
Absolutely! Split processes all receipts on your device using advanced on-device AI. Your receipt photos, personal information, and payment details never leave your phone. We don't store, analyze, or share any of your data. Your privacy is our top priority.
Can I split bills with people who don't have the app?
Yes! That's one of the best features of Split. When you send payment requests, people without the app receive a simple web link where they can pay using their preferred method. No app download required - just click and pay. Perfect for splitting bills with coworkers, family, or anyone.
How does Split handle tax and tip?
Split automatically detects tax and tip on receipts and distributes them fairly among everyone. You can also set custom tip percentages (15%, 18%, 20%, etc.) and Split will recalculate everything. For receipts without tip, you can add it manually and Split will split it evenly or by item.
What if someone doesn't pay?
Split includes built-in payment tracking and friendly reminders. You can see who's paid and who hasn't, and send gentle reminders through the app. For persistent non-payers, you can export the split details to handle it however you prefer. Most people pay within 24 hours!
When will Split be available?
Split is currently in beta testing through TestFlight. We're working hard to make it perfect before the App Store launch. Join our beta to try it early, provide feedback, and help us build the best receipt splitting app ever. The more feedback we get, the faster we can launch!
