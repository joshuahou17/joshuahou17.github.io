Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

At Split, we believe your privacy is fundamental. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our receipt splitting app. We're committed to transparency and protecting your data.

Information We Don't Collect

This is the most important section: We don't collect, store, or process your personal information.

  • Receipt Photos: All receipt processing happens on your device using on-device AI. Your photos never leave your phone.
  • Personal Information: We don't collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personal identifiers.
  • Payment Information: We don't handle payments directly. All payments go through your preferred payment platforms (Venmo, Apple Pay, etc.).
  • Location Data: We don't track your location or collect GPS data.
  • Usage Analytics: We don't track how you use the app or collect analytics data.

How Split Works

Split processes everything locally on your device:

  • Receipt Scanning: Our AI runs entirely on your phone to read receipt text and extract items.
  • Bill Splitting: All calculations happen on your device - no server processing required.
  • Payment Links: We generate payment links that connect directly to payment platforms, bypassing our servers entirely.

Third-Party Services

Split integrates with payment platforms, but we don't control their privacy practices:

  • Payment Platforms: When you use Venmo, Apple Pay, Cash App, or other payment services, their privacy policies apply.
  • App Store: Apple's App Store may collect basic usage data according to their privacy policy.

Data Security

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing to secure on our end. However, we implement best practices:

  • On-Device Processing: All sensitive operations happen locally on your device.
  • No Data Transmission: Your receipt photos and personal information never leave your phone.
  • Secure Payment Links: Payment links use industry-standard encryption.

Children's Privacy

Split is not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you're a parent and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@joshhou.com

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your personal information, there's no data to access, modify, or delete. Your data stays on your device, under your control.

Remember: Your privacy is our priority. We built Split to be private by design - your data never leaves your device.
